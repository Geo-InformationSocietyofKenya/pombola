2016 is Olympic Year!

Kenyan Athletes are the
Most Successful in Africa!

Our Kenyan heroes include:

  • David Rudisha - the current World Record Holder at the 800m and reigning Olympic Champion!
  • Ezekiel Kemboi - reigning Olympic Champion at the 3,000m Steeplechase!
  • Vivian Cheruiyot - World Champion at the 10,000m in Beijing in 2015!

How Kenya compares to
other countries in Africa

Kenya has won more medals at the Summer Olympic Games than any other country in Africa! We have won a total of 86 medals from 13 Olympic Games.

The next best country is South Africa, who have won 76 medals, but they achieved this through taking part in 18 Olympic Games.
